About Us

We're delighted to have you consider Blue Lilly as your home away from home. Nestled in a peaceful neighborhood, our cozy retreat offers the perfect balance of comfort, convenience, and local charm. Whether you're here to explore the vibrant sights of Guwahati or simply to unwind, we've designed every corner of our space to make you feel relaxed and at ease.

From our thoughtfully furnished rooms to our inviting common areas, Blue Lilly is more than just a place to stay—it's a place to connect, recharge, and create memories. With nearby attractions, beautiful natural scenery, and local cultural spots just a short distance away, you'll have everything you need to make your visit truly special.

We can’t wait to welcome you and provide a stay filled with warmth and unforgettable experiences. At Blue Lilly, you’re not just a guest—you’re part of the family.

Enjoy your stay, and let us know if there’s anything we can do to make it even better!
